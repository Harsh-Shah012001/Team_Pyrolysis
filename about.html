We at PP Fuel aim to create a better environment for the generations to come.

We aim to reduce the pollution and harms caused by plastic to our environment. We have three main goals:

  • Reducing plastic dumping in our environment.
  • Recycling plastic into useful fuels.
  • Encouraging and linking Pyrolysis with the world
